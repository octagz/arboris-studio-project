# Roadmap Decision Options

## Option 1: Aggressive Go-to-Market
**Description**: Launch with full feature set, aggressive pricing, and heavy marketing spend to gain market share quickly.

**Investment**: High ($6M Year 1)
**Timeline**: 12 months to market
**Risk Profile**: High risk, high reward

**Key Decisions**:
- Build comprehensive feature set before launch
- Invest heavily in marketing ($2M+ Year 1)
- Competitive pricing below market
- Target enterprise customers immediately

## Option 2: Lean MVP Launch
**Description**: Launch with core features only, focus on product-market fit, iterate based on feedback.

**Investment**: Low-Medium ($3M Year 1)
**Timeline**: 9 months to market
**Risk Profile**: Medium risk, measured growth

**Key Decisions**:
- Ship MVP with 3-5 core features
- Limited marketing spend ($500K Year 1)
- Market-rate pricing
- Start with SMB/mid-market, expand later

## Option 3: Vertical-First Approach
**Description**: Focus on specific industry vertical (e.g., healthcare or finance) with specialized features.

**Investment**: Medium ($4M Year 1)
**Timeline**: 15 months to market
**Risk Profile**: Medium risk, lower competition

**Key Decisions**:
- Build vertical-specific integrations
- Target specific use cases
- Partner with industry leaders
- Expand horizontally after vertical dominance

## Option 4: Partner-Led GTM
**Description**: Co-develop with strategic partners, white-label solution, leverage partner channels.

**Investment**: Medium ($4.5M Year 1)
**Timeline**: 18 months to market
**Risk Profile**: Medium risk, slower but steadier

**Key Decisions**:
- Build partnership-first platform
- API-heavy architecture for integrations
- White-label capabilities
- Co-marketing with partners

## Option 5: Open-Source Strategy
**Description**: Open-source core engine, monetize through hosting and enterprise features.

**Investment**: High ($5.5M Year 1)
**Timeline**: 12 months to market
**Risk Profile**: High risk, viral potential

**Key Decisions**:
- Open-source analytics core
- Build commercial hosting platform
- Enterprise features behind paywall
- Community-driven innovation

## Option 6: Acquisition Play
**Description**: Acquire complementary technology/platform to accelerate development.

**Investment**: Very High ($8M+ Year 1)
**Timeline**: 6-9 months to market
**Risk Profile**: Very High risk, fastest path

**Key Decisions**:
- Identify and acquire target company
- Integrate existing technology
- Retain key talent
- Rapid market entry
